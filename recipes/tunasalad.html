Tuna Salad

Description

An extremely easy and low effort tuna dish that can be made in less than 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna - preferably canned in water
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pepper
  • Salt optional

Steps

  1. Open Canned Tuna and empty it into a bowl
  2. Add one teaspoon of pepper
  3. Add and mix mayonnaise in increments of 1/4th cup until you reach desired consistency
  4. Add pepper and salt to taste
